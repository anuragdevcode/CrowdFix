## 📌 The Vision
Civic infrastructure reporting is traditionally tedious, bureaucratic, and offers zero immediate gratification. **CrowdFix** is a gamified, two-sided mobile ecosystem that removes the friction from civic action. We divide users into **Spotters** (who verify issues via GPS/Photos) and **Fixers** (who resolve issues for bounty points), creating a decentralized workforce for social good.

## 🚀 Technical Stack
* **Frontend:** React Native, Expo
* **Navigation:** Expo Router
* **UI/UX:** Custom high-performance, dark-mode styling optimized for accessibility and one-handed field use.

## 🎮 Core Mechanics (MVP & Roadmap)

### 1. The Dynamic Bounty Market
Issues are not just static pins; they are live bounties. As hazards sit unresolved and gain community upvotes, their point values compound dynamically. Users monitor the map to claim peaking bounties.

### 2. Spotters & Fixers Ecosystem
* **Spotters:** Instantly report and verify issues with frictionless photo capture and exact GPS tagging.
* **Fixers:** Resolve the problem, upload "Before/After" cryptographic proof, and claim the bounty.

### 3. Social Virality Engine
Built-in tools generate highly visual "Before/After" sliders. With one tap, users can push their completed bounties to social media with a branded overlay detailing their stats (Hazard Level, Bounty Earned, Time to Fix), driving organic user acquisition.

### 4. Guilds & Multiplayer Raids (In Development)
Users can form local "Guilds" to compete on city-wide leaderboards. For massive neighborhood cleanups, Fixers can initiate "Raids" where multiple users converge to split a massive point jackpot.

## 💼 The Business Model
CrowdFix is designed to scale via four distinct revenue streams:
1. **B2G Data Sales:** Providing municipalities with real-time, verified heat maps of failing infrastructure.
2. **Corporate Sponsored Raids (CSR):** Brands sponsor "Mega Bounties" for measurable, community-driven PR.
3. **Hyper-Local Lead Gen:** Local businesses subscribe to become "Redemption Zones," trading app points for discounts to drive foot traffic.
4. **Freemium Tier:** Advanced civic analytics and custom cosmetics for Guild leaders.
